# Company Leave Policy

## 1. Casual Leaves (CL)
Employees are entitled to 12 casual leaves per calendar year. Casual leaves are meant for urgent and unforeseen personal work. A maximum of 3 casual leaves can be taken consecutively. Unused casual leaves do not carry forward to the next year.

## 2. Sick Leaves (SL)
Employees are granted 8 sick leaves per year. If an employee is sick for more than 2 consecutive days, a medical certificate must be provided. Unused sick leaves can be accumulated up to a maximum of 20 days.

## 3. Privilege Leaves (PL)
Employees earn 1.5 privilege leaves for every month worked, totaling 18 days per year. Privilege leaves must be applied for at least 7 days in advance.

## 4. Maternity and Paternity Leaves
Female employees are eligible for 26 weeks of paid maternity leave. Male employees are entitled to 2 weeks of paid paternity leave, which must be availed within 6 months of the child's birth.
