# Company Payroll Policy

## 1. Salary Payment Cycle
Salaries are disbursed on the last working day of every month. If the last day of the month falls on a weekend or public holiday, salaries will be credited on the preceding working day.

## 2. Salary Deductions
Standard deductions include Income Tax (TDS), Provident Fund (PF) which is 12% of basic salary, and Professional Tax as applicable per state laws. Any unapproved leaves (Leave Without Pay) will be deducted proportionally from the monthly salary.

## 3. Reimbursements
Employees can claim reimbursements for internet expenses (up to $50/month) and business travel. All claims must be submitted by the 20th of the month with valid receipts to be processed in the same month's payroll.

## 4. Bonus and Variable Pay
Annual performance bonuses are paid out in the month of April, based on the performance review cycle from January to December of the previous year. Variable pay is calculated quarterly and paid in the subsequent month.
